Your preferred application to search suitable Clinical Trials for your medical condition. PharmaLedger is a 29 member blockchain consortium, including 12 global pharmaceutical companies. It aims to enable healthcare innovation for supply chain, clinical trials and health data. The goal of the PharmaLedger project is to deliver a blockchain-based platform for the healthcare sector, using the supply chain, clinicaltrials, and health data as case studies.
